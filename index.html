Delectable Fusions

Stacita and Clendon are both multi-racial fusions who have eaten all over the globe. They are passionate about international foods and especially fusion cuisine. These next-level tortillas have flavor influences from all over the world, such as India, Europe, South America, and more! These are not abuela's tortillas!

Just Desserts

Enjoy tortillas as not just savory, but also sweet. These never-before-seen dessert tortillas infused with cinnamon, sugar, cardamom, cloves, vanilla, or chocolate are the perfect replacement for crepes, pancakes, puff pastries, sweet crispy deep-fried wontons, or any other carb-bomb you can dream up. Go wild!
